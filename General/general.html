General instruction whick make easy and interesting when your going to gym

Items to carry in your gym bag


  • Workout Clothes
    • Athletic shorts or leggings
    • T-shirt or tank top
    • Sports bra (for women)
    • Socks (preferably moisture-wicking)
  • Footwear
    • Athletic shoes
  • Hygiene and Personal care
    • Towel
    • Deodorant
    • Feminine hygiene products (if needed)
  • Gear & Accessories
    • Water bottle
    • Gym gloves
    • Heart rate monitor or fitness tracker
    • Yoga mat
  • Post-Workout Recovery
    • Protein shake or snack
    • Foam roller or massage ball
  • Entertainment & Miscellaneous
    • Headphones
    • Workout playlist
    • Phone or music player
